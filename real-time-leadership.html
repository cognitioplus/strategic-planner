The MOVE Framework

Four pillars of mindful, decisive leadership.

Master the Moment

Cultivate present awareness. Pause, breathe, and ground yourself before reacting to crisis.

  • • 3 Deep Breaths technique
  • • Weekly reflective journaling

Options Generation

Embrace creativity. Involve diverse perspectives to avoid cognitive bias and find unique solutions.

  • • Cross-functional brainstorming
  • • Monthly empathy exercises

Validate Choices

Ensure robust decision-making through structured evaluation and feedback loops.

  • • Decision Matrix (Pros/Cons)
  • • Mentor feedback loops

Execute & Evaluate

Commit to action and monitor outcomes. Adapt strategies in real-time based on data.

  • • Decisive agile execution
  • • Outcome tracking systems
